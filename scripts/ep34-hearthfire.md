# Episode 34: "Hearthfire"

**One job:** Let them breathe. The quietest episode in the series. A day off, a blanket, wildflowers, and the ordinary intimacy of two people who stopped pretending. Then the siphon. The calm before the last storm.
**The moment:** Kissing in the wildflowers while the god beneath them stirs happily. Two people in love in a field that exists because they loved each other.

[Beat — 800ms]

[SFX: Ash — quiet. Processing. A long breath in, held, released slowly] [spot]

{Scene 3 — The wildflower field. Afternoon. The argument. The kiss.}

ASH: The system is arbitrary.
