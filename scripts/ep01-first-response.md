# Episode 1: "First Response"

**One job:** Establish Rune's world — competent, grounded, haunted — then shatter it the moment his hand touches Ash.
**The moment:** Rune touches Ash's chest and the scars ignite. Something locks into place inside his ribcage. The bond begins.

---

{Scene 1 — Darkness. The nightmare. Fire and stone and ancient voices.}

[AMBIENT: dream soundscape — roaring fire, distant chanting, heat and stone] [bed]

[SFX: roaring fire, large-scale. Stone cracking under heat. Ancient chanting, low and rhythmic, rising into a scream] [bed, fade-in]

[DIRECTION: The fire is consuming, enormous — a ritual blaze, not a house fire]

RUNE (inner monologue): The dream again.

[SFX: chanting distorts — voices layering, multiplying] [under]

RUNE (inner monologue): Same fire. Same temple. Same voice I can't see a face for.

[SFX: a hand gripping another — skin on skin — then slipping free] [spot]

[DIRECTION: The grip is desperate — someone trying not to lose someone]

VOICE (deep, desperate, calling through fire): Rune.

[Beat — 800ms]

RUNE (inner monologue): He says my name like he's losing me.

[SFX: sharp gasp — Rune jolting awake] [spot]

---

{Scene 2 — Ambulance interior, moving. Night shift. The siren cuts.}

[AMBIENT: ambulance interior — engine hum on asphalt, low radio chatter] [bed]

[SFX: siren winding down] [fade-out]

RUNE: Yeah. I'm up.

MIKA: You were out for nine minutes. That's a record for you.

RUNE: What've we got?

MIKA: Chest pains, seventy-two-year-old male, 14 Barrow Street. Straightforward.

RUNE: Good. Straightforward is good.

MIKA: You look like you haven't slept in a month.

RUNE: Bad sleep. That's all.

MIKA: Mm-hm.

RUNE (inner monologue): She never buys it. But Mika has a rule — she pushes once, reads the answer, and decides whether it's worth a second push. Tonight she lets it go.

---

{Scene 3 — Apartment building hallway. Elderly patient. Routine call.}

[AMBIENT: apartment building hallway — muffled building hum, fluorescent buzz] [bed]

[SFX: heavy medical bag unzipping] [spot]
[SFX: blood pressure cuff velcro. Portable monitor beeping] [bridge]

RUNE: Mr. Vasquez? I'm Rune, this is Mika. Can you tell me where the pain is?

MR. VASQUEZ (elderly, strained): Left side. Under the arm. Started about an hour ago.

RUNE: Scale of one to ten?

MR. VASQUEZ: Six. Maybe seven when I breathe deep.

RUNE: Okay. I'm going to put this cuff on your arm. Just keep breathing normally for me.

[SFX: cuff inflating] [spot]
[SFX: monitor beeping steadily] [bed]

RUNE (inner monologue): His rhythm's off. I can feel it before the monitor catches up — something in the way his breathing hitches half a beat too early. Training, I tell myself. Just training.

RUNE: Mika, let's get a line started. Mr. Vasquez, we're going to take you in. Probably nothing serious, but we want to be sure.

MR. VASQUEZ: My wife's going to kill me.

RUNE: Tell her it's my fault. I'm very persuasive.

MIKA: He's really not.

[SFX: gurney wheels clicking into position] [spot]

---

{Scene 4 — Rune's apartment. After shift. Small, quiet, alone.}

[SFX: keys in a lock. Door opening and closing] [spot]

[AMBIENT: small apartment, 3 AM — fridge hum, deep quiet] [bed]

RUNE (inner monologue): Home. Which is a generous word for it. A couch, a bed, a shelf of books I keep meaning to finish. The kind of place that says: someone lives here, but not very much.

[SFX: boots dropping onto the floor. Body settling onto a mattress] [spot]

RUNE (inner monologue): The nightmare's been getting worse for three weeks. Same fire. Same voice. Same hand slipping out of mine. I haven't told Mika. I haven't told anyone.

RUNE (inner monologue): The voice in the dream knows my name. Not casually, not curiously — the way someone says it when they're losing you.

[SFX: dispatch radio crackling to life on a bedside table] [spot]

DISPATCH (radio-filtered): All units, off-duty call for any available. Possible injured person, eastern district, abandoned site off Route 9. Any available unit respond.

[Beat — 800ms]

RUNE (inner monologue): My shift ended forty minutes ago. I should be asleep. I should let someone else take it.

[SFX: Rune sitting up. Grabbing keys] [spot]

RUNE (inner monologue): I take it anyway.

---

{Scene 5 — The eastern edge of the city. An abandoned shrine under a highway overpass. Night.}

[SFX: car engine cutting. Door opening] [spot]

[AMBIENT: highway overpass — distant drone overhead, night air, gravel underfoot] [bed]

RUNE (inner monologue): The shrine is half-buried under the overpass. Concrete and weeds and graffiti on the outside. Nobody's been out here in years. The dispatch said a jogger called it in — saw light coming from inside. No light now.

[SFX: pushing through overgrown brush. A rusted gate groaning open] [spot]

RUNE (inner monologue): The air changes the second I step through.

[SFX: footsteps shifting from gravel to stone] [bridge]

[AMBIENT: inside the shrine — muffled highway, faint subsonic hum, warm dead air] [bed, crossfade]

RUNE (inner monologue): It's warm. Not summer warm — *wrong* warm. Like standing next to an oven someone forgot to turn off. The ground is covered in ash. Inches of it. Gray and fine and it doesn't smell like anything burned.

[SFX: careful footsteps through soft ash. Medical bag clinking] [bridge]

RUNE: Hello? Paramedic. Is anyone here?

[Silence — 1500ms]

RUNE (inner monologue): In the center of the shrine, on the ground — a man.

[SFX: Rune's footsteps quickening. Knees hitting stone] [spot]

RUNE (inner monologue): He's unconscious. No clothes. Covered in scars — lines and symbols cut into his skin, old and healed, too precise to be accidental. They look like writing. His skin is hot to the touch. Not feverish — *radiating.*

RUNE: Sir? Can you hear me? I'm a paramedic. I'm going to check your—

[SFX: skin pressing against skin. Then — a sharp, high-frequency tone spiking] [spot]

[DIRECTION: The SFX spike should feel physical — the actors react as if hit by a pressure wave. Ash's next breath is a gasp, not a word]

RUNE (inner monologue): I can't describe what happens next.

[SFX: subsonic hum spikes sharply, then resolves into a deep resonant click] [spot]

[DIRECTION: The hum vibrates through stone, through bone. The click is felt more than heard — something locks into place inside Rune's ribcage, fundamental, irreversible]

RUNE (inner monologue): Something settles behind my sternum. Heavy. Permanent. Like a door I didn't know was there just closed, and the lock engaged from the other side.

[Beat — 800ms]

[SFX: slow, deep breathing] [bridge]

[SFX: sharp inhale] [spot]

[DIRECTION: A sharp inhale from Ash — the first conscious breath. Raw, involuntary]

RUNE (inner monologue): His eyes open. They're black. Not dark brown, not deep gray — black. The whole eye. No white, no iris. Just void.

[Silence — 1500ms]

RUNE (inner monologue): Then they clear. Brown. Human. And he looks at me with an expression I will never forget. Not fear. Not confusion.

[Beat — 800ms]

RUNE (inner monologue): Fury.

ASH (low, raw, an accusation): ...You.

RUNE (inner monologue): He says it like I did this to him. Like my face is the answer to a question he didn't want to ask. Like he's been gone a very long time, and I'm the reason he's back.

[AMBIENT: subsonic hum beneath the shrine — steady, constant] [bed]

RUNE (inner monologue): I don't move. I don't pull my hand away. Something in my chest won't let me.

RUNE (inner monologue): And the thing behind my sternum — the locked door, the turned key — it hums.

[SFX: bond-hum-neutral — low, steady] [bed, fade-out]
