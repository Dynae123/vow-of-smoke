# Episode 37: "Circuit"

**One job:** The final ritual. Ash and Rune open the bond fully — every wall, every memory. The siphon is redirected through them into the Meridian. The circuit closes. The cage holds. The cost is pain beyond language.
**The moment:** The bond opens and they feel everything. A thousand years. A lifetime. The love underneath both. Then the circuit blazes and the pain crests and breaks and there is dawn.

---

{Scene 1 — Cathedral undercrypt. Night. The preparation.}

[AMBIENT: the undercrypt — the oldest room in the city. Carved stone that predates the cathedral above it by a thousand years. Vaulted ceiling lost in darkness. The acoustic is immense — voices carry and return changed. Candles. Dozens of them, in iron holders along the walls. The flicker audible as a collective whisper. And below the stone: the meridian-thrum at its deepest, strongest point. The heart of the network. The central node. The weight of every sacred site in the city converging here, in the bedrock, in the dark] [bed]

[SFX: footsteps — multiple. Shoes on ancient flagstone. Papers rustling. Kaya's voice low, instructing acolytes. The clink of copper discs being placed. Monitoring equipment humming] [spot]

[SFX: Thessa — at the far end. Her voice carrying across the crypt, on the phone. Clipped. No contractions. Giving orders] [spot]

THESSA: Harbor district. Confirm your anchor is set. North quarter. The new node — do not attempt to stabilize. Let it respond naturally. South quarter. Sable is with you. Follow their lead.

[SFX: Mika — near the center. A folding table. Medical equipment: blood pressure cuff, pulse oximeter, IV supplies. Kit open, everything reachable] [spot]

[SFX: bond-harmonic — rising. Two frequencies approaching the center of the room from opposite sides. Converging] [under]

RUNE (inner monologue): The undercrypt smells like candle wax and old stone and copper. The same smell as the night Kaya took us to the Meridian for the first time — a lifetime ago. Before the vow. Before the solstice. Before I knew what the bond would become and how much of me it would take and how little I would mind.

[Beat — 400ms]

RUNE (inner monologue): Kaya's drawn the circuit on the floor in copper wire and chalk. It connects the siphon's remote feeds — relayed through god-keeper tech I don't understand — to the central node beneath our feet. The cathedral god is present. I can't see it. But the air is thicker here than anywhere I've been. The oldest god in the city, awake and watching, the seal on the First Root running through its roots like blood through a heart.

[SFX: Ash — approaching. Boots on flagstone. Rune's breathing catching slightly as Ash enters his space. The scars audibly warm — a faint hiss of heat] [spot]

ASH: The node is strong. Stronger than I expected. The cathedral god has been reinforcing the seal since the solstice. It knows what we are here to do.

KAYA: Can you feel the siphon from here.

ASH: Yes. Distant. But connected. The drain is pulling at the periphery — the harbor node is losing pressure. The north quarter holding. The old sites between them thinning.

[Beat — 800ms]

KAYA: Then we are ready. The ritual sequence is straightforward in principle. Complex in execution.

[SFX: Kaya — at the center. His voice in a register Rune has never heard: older, ritualistic. The cadence of a rite. His hands steady, his voice steady] [spot]

[DIRECTION: Kaya: steady on the surface. Terrified underneath]

KAYA: You stand in the center. Both of you. The copper circuit on the floor channels the siphon's connection through the central node and into the space between you. When the bond opens, the circuit engages. The siphon's drain redirects through you — through the shared circuit — and back into the Meridian. The loop closes. The system stabilizes.

[Beat — 400ms]

KAYA: But the bond must be fully open. Not the managed connection you have maintained. Everything. Every wall must come down. Every channel open. The circuit requires the full capacity of the bond to handle the load. If you hold anything back, the circuit fails and the power has nowhere to go.

[Silence — 1500ms]

RUNE: Every wall.

KAYA: Every wall.

[SFX: Rune — a dry swallow. Throat tight] [spot]

RUNE (inner monologue): Every wall. He means the rooms I keep locked. Not secrets — fears. Am I enough. Will the bond hold. What if I break first. I keep them closed not to hide from Ash but to protect him from the weight of my worry.

[SFX: Ash — stepping close. His hand finding Rune's. Firm grip. The scars hot against Rune's palm] [spot]

[DIRECTION: Through the bond: Ash's own locked rooms. The thousand years. The hunger. The grief he carries for a self he can't fully recover — all behind walls he built to keep Rune from drowning in a millennium of sorrow]

ASH: Rune.

RUNE: Yeah.

ASH: I will not hold back if you won't.

[Beat — 800ms]

RUNE: Deal.

[SFX: Mika — the cuff. Wrapping Rune's arm. The velcro rip. The pump inflating. Her voice steady, professional] [spot]

MIKA: One-twenty over seventy-eight. You're good. Heart rate seventy-two.

RUNE: Normal.

MIKA: Suspiciously normal. You should be terrified.

RUNE: I am.

MIKA: Good. Means your body's lying, not your brain. I'll take it.

[SFX: Mika — her hand on Rune's shoulder. A brief squeeze. Then she lets go] [spot]

[Beat — 400ms]

MIKA: I'll be right here. If your vitals drop, I'm pulling you out.

RUNE: Mika —

MIKA: I don't remember asking your opinion.

{Scene 2 — Cathedral undercrypt. The ritual.}

[AMBIENT: the undercrypt — shifted. Kaya has extinguished the overhead lights. Only candles remain. The copper circuit on the floor catching the flame-light. The acoustic changed — the room is listening. The meridian-thrum rising from beneath the floor, audible now without straining, the heartbeat of the city's oldest sacred site responding to the intent in the room] [bed, crossfade]

[SFX: Ash and Rune — in the center of the copper circuit. Standing close enough that their breathing overlaps. Hands clasped. The scars radiating heat against Rune's palms] [spot]

[SFX: Kaya — at the edge of the circle. His voice beginning the ritual sequence in the old language. The syllables resonant, heavy, felt more than understood] [spot]

[SFX: Thessa — on the phone, voice low. "Commencing." The word traveling outward through the network] [spot]

[SFX: Sable — present through the Meridian. The south quarter node pulsing. Their voice barely audible through the stone: a single word in the divine register. Permission] [under]

RUNE (inner monologue): Kaya speaks the words and the floor hums. The copper wire glows. And between my hands — his hands, warm, the scars hot now, the binding marks lit from inside — the bond begins to open.

[Beat — 400ms]

RUNE (inner monologue): Not the careful managed opening we've practiced. Not the diagnostic connection or the emotional channel or the combat link. Everything. The lock on every door turning at once.

[SFX: bond-harmonic — expanding. The two-frequency hum widening, deepening. The channel between them opening into an ocean. Two frequencies merging into something larger than the room] [under]

[SFX: Ash — a sharp, shuddering breath. Low, involuntary. The exhale of something held too long finally releasing] [spot]

[DIRECTION: Ash: guard dropping. Vulnerable]

RUNE (inner monologue): I feel him.

[Beat — 400ms]

RUNE (inner monologue): Not the surface. Not the temperature or the heartbeat or the emotional weather I've learned to read. Him. The whole span. A thousand years of sealed darkness where time was a sound without rhythm and the only thing that persisted was a dream of a hand he couldn't hold. The rage when he woke — real, enormous, the fury of a being dragged back into consciousness without consent. The terror underneath the rage. The disorientation of a mind built for divinity trapped in a body that didn't know what a car was.

[SFX: bond-harmonic — swelling. A low sustained drone layered with sub-bass pulses. The sound of a circuit widening under load] [under]

[SFX: bond-harmonic — a layered surge. Cold stone. Open air. The lock turning, felt from the other side] [under]

RUNE (inner monologue): And the thing he kept behind the last wall. The thing he locked away even from the bond.

[Beat — 800ms]

RUNE (inner monologue): He was afraid. Not of dying. Not of the hunger or the power or the gods who wanted to use him. He was afraid he wasn't real. That the ritual had hollowed him so completely that what walked around in his body was a mechanism wearing a face. That the feelings he felt for me were the bond's engineering, not his own. That love was a symptom and he was a disease.

[Silence — 1500ms]

RUNE (inner monologue): And then the vow. And the love stayed. And the fear broke. Not disappeared — broke. Like a fever. The mechanism theory couldn't survive the evidence. He chose. Not the bond. Him. He chose and the choosing was the proof and the proof was enough and the fear became a scar instead of a wound. He carries it. He'll always carry it. But he doesn't believe it anymore.

[SFX: Ash — a low sound. Release, not pain. His hands tightening on Rune's. A surge of heat from the scars. The bond-harmonic resonating fully, no distortion] [spot]

KAYA: The circuit is forming. Hold.

[SFX: the bond — Rune's side opening now. His walls going down. The locked rooms. The fears he carried under the paramedic calm] [under]

RUNE (inner monologue): He feels me. All of me. The nightmares — not the shrine dream, the other ones. The ones where the patient dies and I'm standing there and my hands are doing the compressions and the rhythm is right and the technique is right and the patient dies anyway and I go home and I lie awake and I think: what did I miss. What did I miss. What did I miss.

[Beat — 400ms]

RUNE (inner monologue): The guilt I can't name. The thing Mika has seen and I won't let her touch. The feeling that I became a paramedic not because I wanted to save people but because I needed to prove that I could. That every patient is a test and every death is a failure and the grades never average out.

[SFX: Ash — a low, steady breath. Receiving. The bond-harmonic deepening, widening, absorbing] [under]

RUNE (inner monologue): He feels it all. Every patient I lost. The weight of a profession that measures itself in the gap between what you did and what you should have done. The self-doubt under the competence. And alongside it: the love. Not dramatic. Daily. Built from obligation to habit to choice to certainty. The certainty that arrived not as a revelation but as an accumulation. One morning I knew. In my hands. In the part of me that was built for this.

[SFX: bond-harmonic — merged. A single warm tone where two frequencies used to be. Full. Resonant. The circuit humming at capacity, steady, holding] [under]

[DIRECTION: Both: open. Still. Holding]

[Beat — 800ms]

[SFX: Kaya — the next phrase in the old language, tone shifting. The copper wire on the floor humming. A distant cycling whine — the siphon — catching the bond's frequency. Resistance. Then flow] [spot]

[SFX: the Meridian — responding. The thrum beneath the floor rising sharply. Distant nodes steadying, layering in: harbor, north quarter, south quarter. The central node flaring — a deep, enormous resonance filling the undercrypt] [bed]

[SFX: the First Root seal — deep sub-bass vibration below everything. The seal flaring, reinforced. A pressure settling downward. Heavy. Locked] [under]

[DIRECTION: The circuit didn't just stabilize the network — it became the new lock. The bond pressing down on the oldest prison in the city. The seal holds stronger than before]

[Beat — 400ms]

[SFX: pain — without warning. The bond-harmonic spiking into a harsh, piercing frequency. A high sustained tone that distorts and overloads. The sound of a circuit forced wider than it was built to carry] [spot]

[SFX: Rune — a scream. Involuntary, torn from him. His knees buckling. Hands gripping Ash's, knuckles cracking with the force] [spot]

[SFX: Ash — catching Rune. Arms around him. No scream from Ash — a locked jaw, rigid breathing through clenched teeth. His voice stripped raw, surfacing in the archaic register] [spot]

ASH: I have you. I have you.

[SFX: the pain — cresting. The harmonic at maximum distortion. Three seconds of overload —] [under]

[SFX: — breaking through. The frequency dropping from unbearable to steady. Lower. Wider. The hum settling into a new equilibrium. A system that found its capacity and held] [under]

[Silence — 5000ms]

[SFX: breathing — ragged. Both on the floor. Kaya's old-language words trailing off into silence. The copper wire's hum fading to nothing. The ritual complete] [spot]

KAYA: It's done. The siphon is integrated.

[SFX: Mika — the pulse oximeter beeping. Hands on Rune's wrist. The cuff inflating. Clinical. Fast] [spot]

MIKA: Pulse one-ten. Pressure elevated but dropping. Rune. Can you hear me.

[Beat — 800ms]

RUNE: Yeah. I'm — yeah.

MIKA: Follow my finger.

RUNE: Mika. I'm okay.

MIKA: Follow my finger and then you're okay.

[SFX: Mika — penlight clicking. Checking reflexes. The crisp sounds of a field assessment. Rune sighing, holding still] [spot]

[Beat — 400ms]

[SFX: the undercrypt — settling. Candles guttering. The acoustic wider, as if the room has expanded. The meridian-thrum strong and steady — healthy for the first time in days] [bed]

[SFX: Kaya — sliding down the wall. His legs giving out. Papers rustling into his lap. A shaking breath. A quiet, unsteady exhale — the sound of a man trying not to cry] [spot]

[DIRECTION: Kaya: shaking, trying not to cry, pretending he's not]

[SFX: Thessa — on the phone. Her voice unsteady for the first time. Quieter. The formality cracked open] [spot]

THESSA: All sites confirm. The network is stable. The seal... holds. Stronger than before the siphon. All sites confirm.

[Silence — 2500ms]

[SFX: above — a shift in acoustic. The crack in the vault letting in birdsong. Dawn. Morning air moving through the broken ceiling into the undercrypt] [spot]

[DIRECTION: Light through the crack in the vault — not candles, not equipment. Dawn. A single shaft finding the two people on the floor]

[SFX: Ash — lifting his head. His breathing evening out. Arms still around Rune. His voice quiet, wrecked, barely above a whisper] [spot]

ASH: Can you feel it?

[Beat — 800ms]

[SFX: bond-harmonic — the new frequency. Broader than anything before. The Meridian's full pulse carried in it — every node layered in, a bass note threading through the harmonic like a foundation. Wide. Steady. Alive] [under]

RUNE: Everything. I can feel... everything.

[Silence — 5000ms]

[SFX: the undercrypt — dawn settling in. Candles still burning. Monitoring equipment beeping steadily. Mika wrapping up the blood pressure cuff. The meridian-thrum strong and steady beneath it all] [bed, fade-out]
