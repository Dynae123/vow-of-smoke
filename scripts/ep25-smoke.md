# Episode 25: "Smoke"

**One job:** The rescue at the shrine. They find Kaya alive and the summoner's full workspace — including the wall of failed anchors. Rune learns he wasn't the first. The summoner leaves a message: THE LAST GOD DIES AT SOLSTICE. And the shrine itself reveals how deep the damage goes.
**The moment:** The wall of photographs. Failed anchors. Rune sees the faces of the people who came before him — all dead — and understands the full weight of having survived.
