# Episode 29: "The Heart of the City"

**One job:** Selin appears in person. She reveals the full architecture of her plan — the emotional bond was designed to make Ash consume willingly, the vow blocked that, so she's adapted: kill the last god, break the Meridian seal, bind the First Root herself. Ash decides to absorb Selin's stolen power. The battle begins.
**The moment:** "The vow holds. We hold." — Ash committing to absorb four gods' worth of stolen power, trusting the bond and the man on the other end of it to keep him human.
