# Characters — Vow of Smoke

---

## Leads

### RUNE ALESSI
- **Role:** Protagonist, narrator
- **Age:** Late twenties
- **Occupation:** Paramedic
- **Voice:** Warm, slightly rough. Internal monologue voice is rawer, more vulnerable.
- **Core traits:** Grounded, anxious, dedicated. Trained to save people. Runs on caffeine and guilt he can't name. Has a paramedic instinct that borders on supernatural — reads patients before monitors do.
- **Bloodline:** Alessi line — descended from Mediterranean temple priests who migrated north centuries ago. The knowledge was lost within two generations. The sensitivity remained. Rune's affinity manifests as his paramedic instinct.
- **Arc:** Begins as a man who saves strangers for a living. Ends as someone who'd burn the world down for one person. The series tracks the erosion of his professional detachment as the bond with Ash forces him to feel everything he's spent years compartmentalizing.
- **Key relationships:**
  - **Ash** — Anchor bond. Starts as obligation/fear, becomes protective, becomes something neither of them can name, becomes love.
  - **Mika** — Older sister and paramedic partner. His grounding wire to the mundane world.
  - **Kaya** — Reluctant mentor figure. Rune trusts him longer than he should.

### ASH
- **Role:** Deuteragonist, love interest
- **Age:** Ageless appearance, mid-twenties. Actually ~1,000 years old (sealed, no memory of it).
- **Nature:** God-eater — a being created through an ancient ritual to consume dying gods and absorb their power.
- **Voice:** Low, resonant. Ranges from cold menace to raw vulnerability. Fire crackles when he's angry.
- **Core traits:** Volatile, magnetic, dangerous. No memory of who he was before the ritual. Hostile and disoriented early on, learning modern life at frightening speed. Weaponizes sarcasm almost immediately. Beneath the anger: shame about what he is, fear of the hunger, confusion about being cared for.
- **Physical traits:** Covered in ritual scars (binding marks). Body temperature runs high. Shadows bend toward him. Fire responds to his emotions. Selectively invulnerable — divine forces can't harm him, but mundane things (a pan, a knife) can.
- **Arc:** From weapon to person. The central question: can a being made to destroy choose to be human? The Vow of Smoke is the answer — trading unlimited power for a mortal life, godhood for personhood, eternity for a lifetime.
- **Key relationships:**
  - **Rune** — Anchor. Starts as resentment ("leashed to a mortal"), becomes dependence, becomes need, becomes the first thing he's chosen in a thousand years.
  - **Sable** — A dying god. The thing Ash was made to consume. Their scenes are the series' moral crucible.
  - **Thessa** — Sees Ash as an abomination. Her hatred forces Ash to confront what he is.

## Recurring

### MIKA ALESSI
- **Role:** Supporting lead
- **Age:** Early thirties
- **Occupation:** Paramedic (Rune's partner)
- **Relation to Rune:** Older sister
- **Voice:** Warm, grounded, increasingly alarmed as she's pulled into the supernatural.
- **Core traits:** Loyal, pragmatic, no-nonsense. Excellent instincts. Treats Ash like a person before Rune fully does. Gets pulled into the supernatural world and handles it with the same competence she brings to emergency calls.

### DR. IDRIS KAYA
- **Role:** Mentor figure (complicated)
- **Age:** Mid-fifties
- **Occupation:** Professor of comparative mythology, city university
- **Voice:** Measured, academic, with undercurrents of obsession.
- **Core traits:** Knows far more than he shares. Has his own history with the old gods and the god-keepers. Was expelled from the god-keepers ~30 years ago alongside Selin for heretical beliefs about the gods' natural lifecycle. Provides exposition but always holds something back.
- **Key relationship:** Former colleague of Selin (the Summoner). Their shared expulsion and diverging paths is a major plot thread.

### THESSA
- **Role:** Antagonist (principled)
- **Occupation:** Leader (Keeper) of the god-keepers in this city
- **Voice:** Cold, authoritative, righteous fury.
- **Core traits:** Sees Ash as an abomination and Rune as a fool. Her position isn't wrong — god-eaters are dangerous, the ritual is forbidden, the bond will kill Rune. But her solution (containment, control) denies Ash's humanity. She represents the establishment response to a crisis that demands something new.

### SABLE
- **Role:** Supporting (tragic)
- **Nature:** A dying god who takes human form. Ancient being wearing a young face.
- **Voice:** Ethereal, layered. Echo/reverb treatment.
- **Core traits:** Desperate, fading, beautiful. The god of the demolished South Quarter library. Choosing to die on their own terms rather than be consumed. Their relationship with Ash is the series' moral center — the god-eater learning not to eat.

### SELIN (THE SUMMONER)
- **Role:** Primary antagonist (identity hidden until Act IV)
- **Voice:** Distorted/processed until reveal.
- **Core traits:** Former god-keeper, expelled alongside Kaya ~30 years ago. Discovered the sealed god-eater beneath the eastern shrine ~6 years ago. Performed the awakening ritual. Identified Rune as a viable anchor through bloodline research. Believes the gods should die to free the world from divine dependency — but needs their power to achieve this.
