### 1. Fact Consistency with Outline

**Ep23 -- "The God of Dust"**

- **Warning -- Sable's dying timeline: "six hundred years" vs. "nine years."** Sable is described as a god who has "been dying for six hundred years" (ep23, lines 140, 94). But the script also says the library was demolished nine years ago and Sable has been dying for nine years (line 42: "It's been dying for nine years. Alone."). These are not contradictory -- the six hundred years likely refers to Sable's total age, and the nine years to the acute decline. But line 140 ("A god who has been dying for six hundred years") directly states the dying itself has lasted six centuries. Compare with line 42 which says the dying started nine years ago. One of these needs adjusting. The CHARACTERS.md entry says "six centuries" of accumulated memory, which fits the total lifespan, not the dying period.
  - Severity: **Warning**
  - Fix: Line 140 should read something like "A god who has lived for six hundred years and been dying for nine" -- or simply "A god who has been fading for nine years."

- **Note -- Sable's offer vs. outline.** The outline (line 638) says Sable offers "their remaining power to Rune... to strengthen the anchor." The script (line 118) has Sable offering "to strengthen the anchor bond. To buy the time the vow needs." Match is clean.
