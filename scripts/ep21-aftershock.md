# Episode 21: "Aftershock"

**One job:** The summoner's phone call reveals that everything — the bond, the feelings, Rune's role — was engineered. The betrayal isn't what the summoner did. It's the possibility that what Rune and Ash built was always the plan. Ash pulls away. Rune refuses to let him. Then the god-keeper safe house is attacked, proving the summoner isn't waiting anymore.
**The moment:** "I gave him to you so you'd soften him. Make him care. Make him vulnerable. Thank you for that, Rune." — The sentence that poisons everything.
