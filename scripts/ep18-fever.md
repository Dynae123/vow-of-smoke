# Episode 18: "Fever"

**One job:** Rune's condition crosses a line. The pretending ends. Ash says "I need you" and means it without the bond as an excuse. The first kiss — not gentle, not careful, not rational. The emotional midpoint of the series: two people who have run out of reasons not to want each other.
**The moment:** "This is not the bond. I know what the bond feels like. This is me." The sentence that ends the argument they've been having since the shrine.

{Scene 2 — Rune's apartment. Evening. The confrontation.}

[SFX: Ash — already in the kitchen. Standing. Not at the window. In the center of the room. Planted. The posture of someone who has been waiting for hours and has organized their waiting into confrontation] [spot]

[SFX: bond-hum-agitated — sharp. Ash's frequency, not Rune's. The agitation pre-existing, aimed] [under]

[SFX: bond-hum-surge — massive, involuntary. Not the anchor frequency. Not the parasitic draw. Something else. A sound the bond has never made before] [spot]

[DIRECTION: The words land. Ash looks as shocked as Rune — the sentence came from a place below strategy, below the thousand-year-old predator, below the god-eater. The human in him. The part that can love and is terrified of it]

[SFX: Ash's voice — dropping. Barely audible. The resonance thinning to something private, unarmored. The voice of the person he was before the altar] [spot]
