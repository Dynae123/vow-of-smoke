# Episode 20: "The Aqueduct"

**One job:** The act break. The full team descends to protect the aqueduct god. The summoner's trap separates Ash from Rune. Ash alone with a dying god and the full force of the hunger — refuses to feed. Rune fights through collapsed tunnels to reach him. Thessa sees a god-eater choose to be human. The Summoner recalibrates.
**The moment:** "I was made for a lot of things. I am choosing something else." — Ash to the dying god, alone, shaking, with no one to perform for.
