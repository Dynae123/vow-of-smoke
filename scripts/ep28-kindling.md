# Episode 28: "Kindling"

**One job:** The day of the solstice. Full team assembles. The vow's aftermath reshapes Ash's power — more controlled, less hungry, but mortal-vulnerable. Kindling create citywide chaos. The team descends into the cathedral undercrypt. The last god is vast. The final confrontation begins.
**The moment:** Ash takes Rune's hand in the car — no words, no bond-speak, just fingers interlocked. The simplest human gesture, from a man who was a god yesterday.
