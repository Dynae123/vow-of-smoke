# Episode 37: "Circuit"

**One job:** The final ritual. Ash and Rune open the bond fully — every wall, every memory. The siphon is redirected through them into the Meridian. The circuit closes. The cage holds. The cost is pain beyond language.
**The moment:** The bond opens and they feel everything. A thousand years. A lifetime. The love underneath both. Then the circuit blazes and the pain crests and breaks and there is dawn.

---

The acoustic is immense — voices carry and return changed. Candles. Dozens of them, in iron holders along the walls. The flicker audible as a collective whisper. And below the stone: the meridian-thrum at its deepest, strongest point. The heart of the network. The central node. [SFX: footsteps — multiple. Kaya moving between stations he has marked on the stone floor with chalk and copper wire. His shoes on ancient flagstone. Papers in his hands. [SFX: bond-harmonic — rising. Two frequencies approaching the center of the room from opposite sides. RUNE (inner monologue): The undercrypt smells like candle wax and old stone and copper. The same smell as the night Kaya took us to the Meridian for the first time — a lifetime ago. Before the vow. Before the solstice. Before I knew what the bond would become and how much of me it would take and how little I would mind.

[Beat — 400ms]

RUNE (inner monologue): Kaya's drawn the circuit on the floor in copper wire and chalk. But the air is thicker here than anywhere I've been. The oldest god in the city, awake and watching, the seal on the First Root running through its roots like blood through a heart.

[SFX: Ash — approaching. His boots on the flagstone. His scars visible in the candlelight — the audience hears it in Rune's breathing, the small catch when Ash enters his field. The binding marks. The ones that glow. The ones that will carry the Meridian's weight after tonight] [spot]

KAYA: Can you feel the siphon from here.

[Beat — 800ms]

KAYA: Then we are ready. The ritual sequence is straightforward in principle. Complex in execution.

[SFX: Kaya — at the center. His voice in the register Rune has never heard: not the lecturer, not the researcher. Something older. A man who was trained as a god-keeper before he was expelled, speaking in the cadence of a rite he was never supposed to perform. His hands steady. His voice steady. The man underneath, terrified] [spot]

KAYA: You stand in the center. Both of you. The copper circuit on the floor channels the siphon's connection through the central node and into the space between you. When the bond opens, the circuit engages. The siphon's drain redirects through you — through the bond — and back into the Meridian. The loop closes. The system stabilizes.

[Beat — 400ms]

KAYA: But the bond must be fully open. Not the managed connection you have maintained. Everything. Every wall must come down. Every channel open. The circuit requires the full capacity of the bond to handle the load. If you hold anything back, the circuit fails and the power has nowhere to go.

[Silence — 1500ms]

RUNE: Every wall.

KAYA: Every wall.

[SFX: Rune — the swallow. The sound of a man who has shared his nervous system with another person for weeks and still has rooms he keeps locked. Not secrets. Fears. The dark corners where the doubts live — am I enough, will the bond hold, what if I break first. The rooms he keeps closed not to hide from Ash but to protect him from the weight of Rune's worry] [spot]

[SFX: Ash — beside Rune. Close. His hand finding Rune's. The grip sure. Through the bond: his own locked rooms. The thousand years. The hunger. The memories that came back in fragments — the temple, the cedar smoke, the person he was before the ritual erased him. The grief he carries for a self he can't fully recover. All of it, behind walls he built to keep Rune from drowning in a millennium of sorrow] [spot]

RUNE: Yeah.

ASH: I will not hold back if you won't.

[Beat — 800ms]

RUNE: Deal.

[SFX: Mika — the cuff. Wrapping Rune's arm. The velcro rip. The pump inflating. The clinical sound of a sister taking her brother's blood pressure before he opens his soul to a divine network. Her voice: steady. Professional. The voice she uses when the patient is the person she loves most and she cannot let that show] [spot]

MIKA: One-twenty over seventy-eight. You're good. Heart rate seventy-two.

RUNE: Normal.

MIKA: Suspiciously normal. You should be terrified.

RUNE: I am.

MIKA: Good. Means your body's lying, not your brain. I'll take it.

[SFX: Mika — her hand on Rune's shoulder. Brief. The squeeze that contains everything she won't say in front of the god-keepers and the gods and the man her brother loves. The squeeze that says: come back] [spot]

[Beat — 400ms]

MIKA: I'll be right here. If your vitals drop, I'm pulling you out.

RUNE: Mika —

MIKA: That also wasn't a question.

{Scene 2 — Cathedral undercrypt. The ritual.}

[AMBIENT: the undercrypt — shifted. Kaya has extinguished the overhead lights. Only candles remain. The copper circuit on the floor catching the flame-light. The acoustic changed — the room is listening. The meridian-thrum rising from beneath the floor, audible now without straining, the heartbeat of the city's oldest sacred site responding to the intent in the room] [bed, crossfade]

[SFX: Ash and Rune — in the center of the copper circuit. Standing. Facing each other. Close enough that their breathing overlaps. Ash's hands in Rune's hands. The scars warm. The binding marks glowing faintly, responding to the Meridian's proximity] [spot]

[SFX: Kaya — at the edge of the circle. His voice beginning the ritual sequence. Not in English. In something older. The old language — the one the vow was spoken in. The words Rune can't translate but can feel, the way he felt the vow's words, the syllables that rearrange the architecture of the bond] [spot]

[SFX: Thessa — on the phone, relaying to the network. Her voice low. "Commencing." The word traveling from her mouth to the harbor, the north quarter, the south quarter, every sacred site in the city. God-keepers at their posts, anchoring the nodes, holding the network steady for what's about to flow through it] [spot]

[SFX: Sable — not present physically but present through the Meridian. The south quarter node pulsing in response. Their energy feeding into the network. A dying god lending what remains of their strength to keep the system stable during the transition. The echo of Sable's voice, barely audible, carried through the stone: a single word in the divine register that sounds like permission] [under]

RUNE (inner monologue): Kaya speaks the words and the floor hums. The copper wire glows. And between my hands — his hands, warm, the scars hot now, the binding marks lit from inside — the bond begins to open.

[Beat — 400ms]

RUNE (inner monologue): Not the careful managed opening we've practiced. Not the diagnostic connection or the emotional channel or the combat link. Everything. The lock on every door turning at once.

[SFX: bond-harmonic — changing. The familiar two-frequency hum expanding. Opening. The sound equivalent of a door widening into a wall widening into the wall disappearing. The circuit between them going from a channel to an ocean. The harmonic deepening, broadening, the two frequencies merging into something that is both and neither and larger than the room] [under]

[SFX: Ash — a sharp breath. The walls coming down on his side. A thousand years pouring through the bond. Not data. Experience. Darkness. Stone. The sealed chamber. Dreams that lasted centuries. The slow erosion of a self that was stripped by ritual and rebuilt from hunger and rage and the faintest ember of a memory — a hand, warm, slipping away] [spot]

RUNE (inner monologue): I feel him.

[Beat — 400ms]

RUNE (inner monologue): Not the surface. Not the temperature or the heartbeat or the emotional weather I've learned to read. Him. The whole span. A thousand years of sealed darkness where time was a sound without rhythm and the only thing that persisted was a dream of a hand he couldn't hold. The rage when he woke — real, enormous, the fury of a being dragged back into consciousness without consent. The terror underneath the rage. The disorientation of a mind built for divinity trapped in a body that didn't know what a car was.

[SFX: the bond — wider. Ash's memories arriving not as narrative but as sensation. Cold stone against skin for centuries. The first taste of air that wasn't sealed-chamber air. The shock of Rune's hand on his chest in the ashes. The lock turning — felt from the other side. Not an anchor engaging. A man waking up] [under]

RUNE (inner monologue): And the thing he kept behind the last wall. The thing he locked away even from the bond.

[Beat — 800ms]

RUNE (inner monologue): He was afraid. Not of dying. Not of the hunger or the power or the gods who wanted to use him. He was afraid he wasn't real. That the ritual had hollowed him so completely that what walked around in his body was a mechanism wearing a face. That the feelings he felt for me were the bond's engineering, not his own. That love was a symptom and he was a disease.

[Silence — 1500ms]

RUNE (inner monologue): And then the vow. And the love stayed. And the fear broke. Not disappeared — broke. Like a fever. The mechanism theory couldn't survive the evidence. He chose. Not the bond. Him. He chose and the choosing was the proof and the proof was enough and the fear became a scar instead of a wound. He carries it. He'll always carry it. But he doesn't believe it anymore.

[SFX: Ash — the sound. Low. Not pain. The release of a man who has been holding a thousand years of weight and just handed it to someone and the someone didn't buckle. His hands tightening on Rune's. The scars blazing. The bond carrying everything and the everything fitting because the circuit was built for this] [spot]

[SFX: the bond — Rune's side opening now. His walls going down. The locked rooms. The fears he carried under the paramedic calm] [under]

RUNE (inner monologue): He feels me. All of me. The nightmares — not the shrine dream, the other ones. The ones where the patient dies and I'm standing there and my hands are doing the compressions and the rhythm is right and the technique is right and the patient dies anyway and I go home and I lie awake and I think: what did I miss. What did I miss. What did I miss.

[Beat — 400ms]

RUNE (inner monologue): The guilt I can't name. The thing Mika has seen and I won't let her touch. The feeling that I became a paramedic not because I wanted to save people but because I needed to prove that I could. That every patient is a test and every death is a failure and the grades never average out.

[SFX: Ash — receiving. Through the bond: the god-eater feeling a mortal man's accumulated grief. Every patient Rune lost. The weight of a profession that measures itself in the gap between what you did and what you should have done. The self-doubt that lived under the competence like rot under paint. And alongside it, woven through it: the love. Not the dramatic love. The daily love. The love that built itself from obligation — I found him, he's my responsibility — to habit — his tea, his books, his scars I check each morning — to choice — I chose this, I chose him, I choose — to certainty. The certainty that arrived not as a revelation but as an accumulation. One morning he knew. The way he knows a patient's prognosis. In his hands. In the part of him that was built for this] [under]

[SFX: bond-harmonic — the two frequencies no longer separate. Merged. A single sound that contains both voices. The circuit fully open. [Beat — 800ms]

[SFX: Kaya — the next phrase. The old language shifting. The ritual engaging the siphon's connection. The copper wire on the floor pulsing. The cathedral god waking fully, its presence filling the undercrypt like water filling a basin, enormous and old and welcoming] [bed]

[SFX: the First Root seal — deep. Below everything. A vibration the audience feels more than hears. The seal on the cage flaring. Reinforced. The circuit didn't just stabilize the network. [SFX: pain — it arrives without warning. Immense. The cost of becoming a conduit for a divine network. Every nerve in both of them lighting up simultaneously. Not damage. Expansion. The body's circuitry forced wider than it was built to carry. The bond screaming with the load. The harmonic spiking into a frequency that is beautiful and terrible] [spot]

[SFX: Ash — holding him. Arms around Rune. ASH: I have you. I have you.

[SFX: the pain — cresting. The circuit finding its capacity. The bond stretching to hold the network's weight and the weight is enormous and for three seconds — three seconds that last a century — the question is whether the circuit will hold or break and the answer is physics and love in equal measure and neither one is enough alone but together —] [under]

[SFX: the crest breaking. The pain reaching its peak and passing through. The circuit stabilizing. The bond settling into its new capacity. The harmonic dropping from the unbearable frequency to something lower, steadier, wider. The sound of a system that has found its new equilibrium. The hum of a network running on two people and the two people holding] [under]

[Silence — 5000ms]

[SFX: breathing — two people. Ragged. On the floor of the undercrypt, Ash holding Rune, both of them kneeling, the copper circuit around them glowing faintly and then dimming to nothing as the connection becomes organic, no longer needing the physical scaffold. [SFX: Mika — the pulse oximeter beeping. Her hands on Rune's wrist. The cuff inflating. Clinical. Fast. The paramedic doing her job while her brother breathes against a god-eater's chest on the floor of a cathedral basement] [spot]

MIKA: Follow my finger.

MIKA: Follow my finger and then you're okay.

[SFX: the undercrypt — settling. The candles guttering. The acoustic changed — wider, somehow. As if the room has expanded. The heartbeat of a network that was dying and just found its new heart] [bed]

[SFX: Kaya — leaning against the wall. His legs giving out quietly. The academic who saved the world with a theory and a dead language and a god who told him to stop thinking of wounds as diseases] [spot]

[SFX: Thessa — on the phone. Her voice. Changed. Not the Keeper's pronouncement register. Something rawer. Something that sounds, for the first time in thirty-eight episodes, uncertain and grateful in equal measure] [spot]

THESSA: All sites confirm. The network is stable. The seal... holds. Stronger than before the siphon. All sites confirm.

[Silence — 2500ms]

[SFX: the ceiling — the broken part. The undercrypt's ancient damage, the crack in the vault that no one repaired because it predates the cathedral. And through it: light. Not candlelight. RUNE: Everything. I can feel... everything.

[Silence — 5000ms]

[SFX: the undercrypt — dawn. The candles still burning. The monitoring equipment beeping steadily. Mika wrapping up the blood pressure cuff. Kaya wiping his face.
