# Episode 14: "The Spring"

**One job:** The team prepares for the spring operation. The pressure cracks something open between Rune and Ash — the bathroom scene, Ash's fingers on Rune's jaw, the first touch that asks a question instead of answering one. Mika refuses to be left behind. They descend into the tunnels and find someone already there.
**The moment:** Ash's hand on Rune's jaw. Not a grab. A question. Rune's internal goes quiet for the first time in the series — just breath, just contact, just the bond singing.

---

{Scene 1 — Rune's apartment. Morning. Battle planning.}

[AMBIENT: apartment morning — multiple voices, maps rustling, Kaya's papers, mugs, the controlled chaos of people preparing for something dangerous] [bed]

[SFX: Kaya spreading a map — old paper, crackling at the folds. Pencil markings] [spot]

KAYA: The underground spring sits beneath the old market district. The entrance point is through the basement of a decommissioned water treatment plant — municipal records show it was sealed in 1987, but the seal was compromised. The tunnel system connects to the natural cave network that feeds the spring.

RUNE: How deep?

KAYA: The spring chamber itself is approximately forty meters below street level. The descent is through a series of switchback tunnels — some carved, some natural. The footing is unstable in sections. Water seepage throughout.

[SFX: Mika setting down a medical kit — heavy, stocked. Zippers and buckles] [spot]

MIKA: I'm bringing a full trauma kit. If we're going into a cave system with unknown hostiles, someone's getting hurt and it's not going to be because we forgot gauze.

RUNE: Mika, you don't have to —

MIKA: Stop.

RUNE: It's dangerous. What we saw at the park was —

MIKA: You're my partner. I don't leave my partner. That's it. That's the whole argument. Move on.

[SFX: Rune's mouth closing. The conversation ending because Mika ended it] [spot]

RUNE (inner monologue): She packed the kit before she got here. She already decided. The argument was a formality.

[SFX: phone buzzing — a text. Kaya checking] [spot]

KAYA: Thessa is sending two acolytes. They will cover the secondary exits from the tunnel system. They will not enter the spring chamber.

RUNE: Because of Ash.

KAYA: Because Thessa's ceasefire has limits and placing her people in a confined space with a god-eater whose hunger is escalating is beyond those limits. Her words, relayed through a third party, because the god-keepers still will not contact you directly.

ASH: Their caution is not unreasonable.

[Beat — 400ms]

RUNE (inner monologue): He says it flat. No edge. The honesty of someone who knows what he is and has stopped pretending the danger isn't real.

KAYA: The timing. The summoner's phone call to Rune places the ritual at the spring within two days — which means tomorrow. If the pattern holds, the ritual will begin at the site several hours before the actual kill. There will be preparation. Practitioners. The opportunity is to interrupt before the god is fatally weakened.

ASH: The opportunity is also a trap.

KAYA: Almost certainly.

ASH: Then we are walking into it deliberately.

RUNE: You got a better idea?

[SFX: Ash's silence — not hostile. Considering] [under]

ASH: No.

[SFX: maps rustling. The quiet industry of people preparing to go underground] [under]

{Scene 2 — Rune's apartment. Afternoon. The bathroom.}

[AMBIENT: apartment, late afternoon — quieter now, Kaya and Mika gone, the planning done, the waiting begun. Clock ticking. Traffic muted] [bed]

[SFX: bond-hum-agitated — low, building. Not Rune's frequency — Ash's, transmitted through the connection] [under]

RUNE (inner monologue): The apartment empties and what's left is the waiting and the hum and Ash. He's been pacing since Kaya left. Living room to kitchen. Kitchen to hallway. The same circuit, the same heavy footsteps, the shadows at his feet restless and dark.

[SFX: Ash's footsteps — pacing, deliberate, the rhythm of someone fighting something internal] [bridge]

RUNE (inner monologue): Through the bond I can feel the hunger building. Three dead gods' worth of unclaimed power still dissipating through the Meridian and every particle of it is pulling at him. It's been building for days. The sinkhole residue. The grove. The temple. All of it floating in the earth below us like ash in water and his body wants it the way a starving man wants bread.

[SFX: footsteps stopping — bathroom doorway] [spot]

RUNE (inner monologue): He goes into the bathroom. I hear the faucet run. Then stop. Then nothing.

[SFX: faucet — on, then off. Silence] [spot]

[Beat — 800ms]

RUNE (inner monologue): The bond spikes. Not pain. Not anger. Something worse — the sound of someone holding on with both hands and feeling their grip slip.

[SFX: Rune's footsteps — quick, to the bathroom doorway] [spot]

[AMBIENT: bathroom — small space reverb, fluorescent light buzz, mirror, tile echo] [bed, crossfade]

RUNE (inner monologue): He's standing at the sink. Both hands on the porcelain. Knuckles white. His head is down and his breathing is wrong — too controlled, too deliberate, the breathing of someone actively preventing themselves from doing something.

[SFX: Ash's hands on porcelain — grip tightening. Knuckles, tension] [spot]

RUNE (inner monologue): In the mirror behind him, the shadows are moving on their own. Reaching. Not toward me — toward the floor. Toward the earth below. Toward the power.

[SFX: ash-shadows-stir — heavy, pulling downward. Mirror vibrating faintly in its frame] [under]

ASH: I can feel it. All of it. The power from the dead sites. It is calling.

[Beat — 400ms]

ASH: And tomorrow we go to the spring. Where another god will be dying. Where the hunger will be —

[SFX: porcelain cracking under Ash's grip — a thin fracture line] [spot]

RUNE: Ash.

[SFX: Rune's hand on Ash's back — between the shoulder blades. Fabric. Warmth. Contact] [spot]

[SFX: bond-hum-surge — the same push as the sinkhole, Rune anchoring, routing himself through the connection] [under]
[DIRECTION: The cost is immediate — Rune feels the drain, the copper taste, the edges of his vision thinning]

RUNE (inner monologue): I push. The same instinct as the sinkhole — not a command, not control. Just me. The feeling of steady ground. Of hands that hold. I push it through the bond and I taste copper and my vision greys at the edges and I do not let go.

[SFX: Ash's breathing changing — the controlled deliberation breaking into something more human. Shoulders dropping] [under]

[SFX: bond tone settling — the agitation smoothing, not gone but banked] [under]

ASH (rough): Stop. You are —

RUNE: I know.

ASH: You are burning yourself to keep me stable.

RUNE: That's the job.

[SFX: Ash turning — the shift of weight, fabric against the sink edge. Now facing Rune] [spot]
[DIRECTION: Bathroom-close. Mirror-close. The space between them measured in inches]

ASH: No. That is a choice. There is a difference.

[Silence — 1500ms]

RUNE (inner monologue): He is very close. The bathroom is small and he has turned to face me and his back is against the sink and my hand has moved from his shoulder blade to somewhere that is neither his shoulder nor his chest and we are standing in the four square feet of tile between the sink and the door.

[SFX: bond-hum-warm — live wire quality, the frequency of proximity and intent] [under]

RUNE (inner monologue): His eyes are dark but not black. The hunger is there — banked, held — but what's on his face isn't hunger. It's something slower. Something that looks at me the way I've been looking at him for days and not admitting it.

[SFX: Ash's hand rising — slow. The sound of sleeve fabric shifting, the faintest brush of air] [spot]
[DIRECTION: Like approaching something that might bolt. A question, not a statement]

[SFX: fingertips on jaw — skin contact. Light. Precise. The bond responding — a flare, warm, spreading from the point of contact through the chest] [spot]

RUNE (inner monologue): His fingers on my jaw.

[SFX: bond-hum-warm — blooming, opening, a sound finding its resonance] [under]

RUNE (inner monologue): Not a grab. A question. His thumb at the hinge of my jaw and his fingers along the bone and the touch is so light I could step back and it would end. He's giving me the exit. The way someone does when they've never been allowed to want something and don't know if wanting is permitted.

[SFX: Rune's breath — a caught inhale] [spot]

RUNE (inner monologue): I close my eyes. I don't decide to. They close. And for the first time since the shrine my internal voice stops talking. No observations. No medical metaphors. No cataloguing. Just the feeling of his hand on my face and the bond singing between us and the heat of him and the bathroom light humming above us and his breathing and my breathing and the silence where all my words used to be.

[Silence — 2500ms]

[SFX: knock at the apartment door — three sharp raps. Mika's voice, muffled through the front door] [spot]

MIKA (through door): Rune. I brought the bloodwork kit. Open up.

[SFX: the moment breaking — Ash's hand dropping. A step back. The sink edge catching his hip] [spot]

[SFX: bond tone — the live wire quality dimming but not gone. Residual warmth] [under]

[Beat — 800ms]

RUNE (inner monologue): His hand drops. The air where his fingers were.

RUNE: I should get the door.

ASH: Yes.

[SFX: Rune's footsteps leaving the bathroom. Hallway. The distance growing] [bridge]

RUNE (inner monologue): I open my eyes and he is looking at me the way he looked at the city from the rooftop. Like something he did not expect to find. Like something he is afraid to keep.

{Scene 3 — Rune's apartment. Evening. The preparation.}

[AMBIENT: apartment evening — Mika drawing blood, clinical sounds layered over domestic space, bond-hum-warm lingering faintly] [bed]

[SFX: tourniquet snapping. Alcohol swab. Needle and vacutainer — the clinical sounds of a blood draw] [spot]

MIKA: Hold still. Fist for me.

RUNE: I know how to give a blood sample, Mika.

MIKA: Then stop fidgeting.

[SFX: blood filling the vacutainer — the subtle change in sound as the tube fills] [under]

MIKA: Two more tubes. CBC, CMP, coag panel. I'll run them at the hospital lab under a patient alias.

RUNE: You're forging lab orders.

MIKA: I'm doing an unauthorized blood draw on my brother in his apartment while a supernatural entity reads a book in the next room. The lab orders are the least illegal part of my evening.

[SFX: Ash turning a page — barely audible from the living room, but present] [under]

[Beat — 400ms]

MIKA (quieter): He's listening.

RUNE: He's always listening. The bond means —

MIKA: I know what the bond means. You told me. I'm asking if he can hear us right now through the wall.

RUNE: Probably.

MIKA: Good. Then he hears this: whatever's happening tomorrow, you are not expendable. You don't get to burn yourself out playing anchor for someone who needs you alive. If you go down in those tunnels, I'm pulling you out. Both of you if I can. But you first. Do you understand me?

[SFX: second vacutainer filling. The clinical process continuing under the emotional weight] [under]

RUNE: Mika.

MIKA: Do you understand me.

RUNE: Yeah. I understand you.

[SFX: needle withdrawal. Cotton ball pressed. Medical tape tearing] [spot]

MIKA: Hold pressure. Two minutes.

[SFX: Mika labeling tubes — marker on glass, the scratch of writing] [spot]

MIKA: Flashlight. Extra batteries. Water. First aid beyond what I'm carrying?

RUNE: I'll pack a secondary kit. Compact.

MIKA: The tunnels — Kaya said the footing's unstable. Boots, not shoes.

RUNE: Yeah.

MIKA (pausing): Are you scared?

RUNE: Of the tunnels?

MIKA: Of him. Of what happens if he can't hold it tomorrow. If the hunger is too strong and you're standing next to a god-eater who decides to eat.

[Silence — 1500ms]

RUNE: I'm not scared of him.

MIKA: What are you scared of?

[Beat — 800ms]

RUNE: That I won't be enough. That I'll push through the bond the way I did at the sinkhole and it won't be enough and he'll have to choose between the hunger and — and I won't be enough.

[SFX: Mika capping the last tube. Setting it in the carrier. Zipping the kit] [spot]

MIKA: You're always enough. You just never believe it. Bandage comes off in an hour.

[SFX: Mika standing. Gathering her things. The efficiency of someone who has said what she needs to say] [spot]

{Scene 4 — The tunnels. Night. The descent.}

[AMBIENT: water treatment plant basement — industrial echo, dripping, old concrete, the smell of damp described through sound: water on stone, rust, ventilation groaning] [bed]

[SFX: metal door forced open — old hinges, screaming. Dust falling. Flashlight click] [spot]

RUNE (inner monologue): The water treatment plant is at the edge of the old market district. The basement is concrete and rust and the kind of dark that feels occupied. Kaya's map says the passage is behind a maintenance wall. It is. The wall has been recently moved — scuff marks on the floor, the bolts clean where everything else is corroded.

[SFX: maintenance panel pulled away — metal on concrete, scraping. The tunnel behind it breathing cooler air] [spot]

[AMBIENT: tunnel entrance — the sound changes immediately, narrower, wetter, the echo of deep stone] [bed, crossfade]

ASH: The god-keepers are in position.

RUNE: You can feel them?

ASH: I can feel everything within a quarter mile of the Meridian. The acolytes are at the north and south exits. They are afraid. It does not impair them.

MIKA: How comforting.

[SFX: three sets of footsteps entering the tunnel — boots on wet stone, different weights and gaits] [bridge]

RUNE (inner monologue): The tunnel narrows fast. Carved stone gives way to natural rock. The ceiling drops. The air gets colder and wetter and the flashlight catches the shine of water running in thin sheets down the walls.

[SFX: descent — boots on uneven stone, some gravel, occasional slip-catch. Water trickling alongside] [bridge]

MIKA: Watch the step here. The stone's undercut.

RUNE: I see it.

[SFX: Ash stopping — his footsteps cutting off. Then the shadows change] [spot]

ASH: Wait.

[SFX: ash-shadows responding — pooling, reaching forward into the dark ahead. The sound of something vast and purposeful rearranging itself] [spot]
[DIRECTION: Ash is using his power. The shadows part like curtains, creating a channel of visibility in absolute darkness]

RUNE (inner monologue): The dark in front of us peels back. Not light — the absence of dark. The shadows pulling away from the tunnel walls and floor, clearing a path that extends ahead of us farther than my flashlight could reach. The first time he's used his power in service of the group. Not combat. Not survival. Practical. He's making a path so we can see where we're walking.

MIKA: That's new.

ASH: It is not new. I have simply not had a reason before.

[SFX: footsteps resuming — three sets, deeper into the earth. Water dripping from a greater height] [bridge]

RUNE (inner monologue): The tunnel descends in switchbacks. Right, then left, then right. The temperature drops. The water sound grows — not the thin trickle from the walls anymore. Something larger below us. A current.

[SFX: the tunnel opening — the echo shifting, expanding. The sound of a larger space ahead] [spot]

RUNE (inner monologue): And underneath the water sound, something else. Barely audible. Rhythmic.

[SFX: chanting — distant, muffled by stone, multiple voices in an old language. The rhythm deliberate, ritual] [under]
[DIRECTION: Not close enough to make out words. But close enough to know it's human voices doing something they've practiced]

MIKA (whispering): Is that —

RUNE (whispering): Yeah.

ASH (low): Someone is already here. The ritual has begun.

[SFX: the chanting continuing — distant, rhythmic, coming from below. Water rushing. The bond-hum-agitated building in Ash's frequency] [under]

[AMBIENT: deep tunnel — the chanting below, water, stone, three people standing at the edge of something ancient and immediate] [bed, fade-out over episode end]

RUNE (inner monologue): His fingers on my jaw. I can still feel it. And we are walking into the dark, and I am following a god-eater into the earth, and the people who planned all of this are waiting for us, and I think I would follow him anywhere.
