# Episode 30: "Solstice"

**One job:** THE CLIMAX. Rune takes the blow. The vow catches and redirects the power through the bond back into the Meridian. The network flares. Selin is stripped. The last god speaks. Ash and Rune survive, holding hands in the dark.
**The moment:** "The chain broke. The anchor held. The vow was worthy." — The last god's verdict, delivered in the voice of the city itself.
