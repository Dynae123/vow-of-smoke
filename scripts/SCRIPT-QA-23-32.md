### NEW PATTERN: "The harmonic/circuit/bond carried/told/translated" -- CRITICAL

**Count: 20 instances across eps 29-32.**

This is the single most noticeable new pattern in the post-vow episodes. The construction "[bond mechanism] [communication verb] it as [sensation]" appears ~5 times per episode in eps 29-32. Examples:
- "The harmonic translated it as alertness" (ep32 line 14)
- "The circuit carried it" (ep31 line 248)
- "The bond told him" (ep32 lines 72, 294, 358)
- "The harmonic carried the frequency of" (ep32 line 326)

The bond was always described in SFX lines, but the pre-vow episodes used variety. Post-vow, the writer settles into a mechanical "[bond] [verb] [sensation]" template that flattens the language.

**Severity: CRITICAL.** This is the batch's most significant construction problem. 20 uses in 4 episodes creates an audible pattern that makes the SFX directions feel like copy-paste. Needs reduction to ~10-12 total, with at least half replaced by direct sensory description or varied framing.

### NEW PATTERN: "shadows at his feet" -- Warning

In the pre-vow episodes (23-26) where the shadows are plot-active, this is justified. In the post-vow episodes where the shadows are described as "dim but present" (ep30) and barely functional, the phrase persists at nearly the same frequency.

**Severity: Warning.** Reduce by 5-6 in the post-vow episodes. The shadows should appear less frequently as Ash's humanity solidifies. Ep32 still has 1 instance -- appropriate for the new status quo.

**Severity: Note.** "Love, misapplied" is the only line that reads as a potential voice bleed from Rune's register into Ash's. Minor.
