# Episode 24: "Revelation"

**One job:** Sable's warning — "closer than you think" — triggers a paranoid investigation of everyone in the inner circle. Kaya connects the summoner to his own past. Then Kaya disappears, and the evidence he left behind points to a truth he couldn't bear to speak aloud.
**The moment:** Kaya's office — door open, shelves toppled, chalk ritual circle on the floor, blood. Kaya is gone.

The shape of a person. The god-keepers had schisms. Expulsions. People who left the order and took their knowledge with them. Kaya was one of them.

[Silence — 2500ms]

[SFX: Kaya — a long exhale. Deliberate. The breath of a man deciding how much to put down] [spot]

[DIRECTION: Kaya's voice when he resumes is stripped of the lecture cadence. Shorter sentences. The academic scaffolding dropping]

KAYA: I have been thinking about this. Since Sable's warning. Since the modified ritual. Since the symbol I could not place.

KAYA: The colleague — we were close. More than colleagues. We studied together. Lived together. Shared a belief in the natural lifecycle. When the expulsion came, we both lost everything.

[Beat — 400ms]

KAYA: I went to the university. Found a way to continue the work through academia. Publish, teach, keep the knowledge alive in a form the modern world could accept.

[SFX: bond-hum — Rune's frequency tightening. The pattern forming before the words do] [under]

Controlling the release of power. Harvesting it. Using it. Someone who believed the gods should die naturally — and then decided nature was too slow.

RUNE: Kaya. Who are they.

[SFX: Kaya — picking up his glasses. Putting them back on. The gesture of a man rebuilding his composure one frame at a time] [spot]

KAYA: I need to confirm. The symbol — the pre-human element in the modified ritual — I believe I know its origin. But I need to verify against the restricted texts. Thessa's archive. There are documents I have not yet been granted access to.

RUNE: Kaya.

KAYA: I will not say the name until I am certain. You understand — if I am wrong, the accusation alone would destroy what remains of the alliance. If I am right —

[Beat — 400ms]

[DIRECTION: Kaya's voice breaks on the next line — not dramatically. A fracture. The composure cracking along a thirty-year-old fault line]

KAYA: If I am right, it means someone I loved very much became the thing I was afraid they would become. And I need to see the proof before I can say it.

[Silence — 2500ms]

[SFX: bond-hum — Ash's frequency carrying the sensation to Rune. Not empathic bleed — intentional sharing. He wants Rune to feel it] [under]

RUNE (inner monologue): He opens the bond and it hits me. Not the warm hum. Not the bond itself. Something beneath it. Vast and slow and without thought. [SFX: Rune — a sharp exhale through his nose. Recalibrating] [spot]

RUNE: How much time do we have. Before the cage fails.

[Silence — 1500ms]

RUNE (inner monologue): Not months. We're on the wrong side of every timeline. The bond drain — weeks, Sable said. The Meridian cage — less than months. The summoner — accelerating. And Kaya is walking to his office alone to confirm the name of the person who started all of this, because he needs to look at the proof one more time before he admits that the person he loved is the one dismantling the world.
